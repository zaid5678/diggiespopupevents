Who We Are

Caribbean Cocktail Culture, Brought to Your Door

Diggies Pop Up Events brings the energy, flavour, and vibes of Caribbean cocktail culture to your doorstep. Whether it's a rum masterclass that takes your taste buds on a journey or a full pop-up bar experience at your next event, we're here to make it unforgettable.

Founded with a passion for great drinks and even better company, Diggies is all about creating moments — the kind you talk about long after the last glass is empty. From intimate masterclasses to large-scale events, we tailor every experience to bring people together over world-class cocktails.
